/ CULTIVATING TALENTS IN TECHNOLOGY /

Ristek Fasilkom UI is an independent organization managed by students of
of Faculty of Computer Science Universitas Indonesia (Fasilkom UI)

which is aimed to facilitate students’ skills and interest in the field of technology.

/ MISSION /

Ristek Fasilkom UI facilitates students' skills and interests by creating eight different Special Interest Groups where they can join and learn. We provide qualified and structured training in purpose to create skillful members. Here, we build a product development culture and also encourage our members to take part in competitions held in the field of technology. All Ristek Fasilkom UI's activities are organized to improve knowledge about technology. Along with it, we build a collaborative environment between members, alumni, elements, and society.
